The Bird Has Left Its Nest

In the name of Allah, the Most Gracious, the most Merciful.

As I noted on the CMWEB 110 homepage, the assignments on this website serve as reference to the fundamental elements, based on web standards, of HTML and CSS. With each assignments being based on the work done from the previous assignments, they add one or two new concepts and features, allowing one to see a guided, piece-by-piece construction of a complex webpage. CMWEB 120 continues this idea, starting right from where the final assignment from CMWEB 110 left off.
