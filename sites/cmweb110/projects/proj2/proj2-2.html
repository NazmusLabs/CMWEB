People are Circles

In the name of Allah, the Most Gracious, the most Merciful.

Did you know, by an unspoken, de-facto design standard, profile pictures of people online are now depected in a circular frame? ðŸ˜® I remember this shift in design gradually happen right before my eyes. In early 2010s, user profile pictures were in standard square frames. Then, Facebook released a feature called "Chatheads" for Messenger users on Android. That triggered a slow shift in design philosophy in which each app and online services started showing user profile pictures in a circular frame. It took many years, but now, it's hard to find an app or service that will show your or yoru friends' profile picture in a square frame.

There is a practical benefit to this, it must be noted; this approach allows users to instantly differenciate on-screen items that are user profiles versus other elements such as a photo or video thumbnail. ðŸ˜‰
