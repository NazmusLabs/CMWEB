Totally Creative Domain Name

Oh, Baby, What a Plan!

Okay, so I looked up the hosting plans available for individuals at https://www.hostgator.com/web-hosting, as required by this assignment, and to my surprise (albeit a pleasent one), the names of the different tiers of plans are adorable. :)

So, the two lowest tiered hosting plans on Hostgator is the "Hatchling Plan" and the "Baby Plan". And the next tier up is called, simply, the "Business Plan". From the looks of it, I like the "Baby Plan", and not just because of it's name (well a little). For just over a dollar more than the "Hatchling Plan" I am able to have unlimited domains rather than having a single domain. The added flexibility makes this a lot more valuable. On the other hand I really don't need dedicated IP or SEO tools any time soon; so, I can pass on the "Business Plan" and save a few bucks!
