The New Champlain Bridge (2019)

A 21st Century Replacement

Construction of the new Champlain bridge began on June 16, 2015. Two years later, on June 24, 2019, the bridge opened to northbound/westbound traffic on. The new bridge bridge replaces the previous 57-year-old Champlain bridge.


Midnight Glow

No matter how late in the night it may be, the new Champlain bridge keeps its colorful lights brightly lit to welcome travelers and residents to the city of Montreal


The Old and the New

As I sit in the sunset to capture this beautiful silhouette of the bridge that connects the city of Montreal to my suburb home, I marval at the two generations of mega-structures, the old being replaced by the new.
