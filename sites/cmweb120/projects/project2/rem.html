REM Metro Construction Progress 2020

Montreal Commuter Trains

Montreal has many commuter lines. The REM will partially replace some routes; it will completely replace the Deux-Montagnes commuter line.


Green Line Metro

This is a station of the Montreal green subway line. The REM will intersect with the green line at McGill Station.


Construction Has Begun

Construction of the most advanced rapid transit system in North America has commenced! This fully automated, state-of-the-art light raile metro is projected to span more than 62Kkm of tracks, servicing major parts of the Greater Montreal Area.
