Color Schemes for Client's Website

Click here to return to the main page for this lab.

Since I chose one of my close friends from Montreal to be my client for the project, I had a relatively good idea of the colors he would want for his website. Therefore, on our first meeting, I presented to him the colors I thought he would most likely want to go with and asked him to confirm or suggest changes. As sure enough, the colors I picked were spot on. However, I did have one more question I needed to ask with regards to the site's overall color theme.

My friend created this logo for his personal brand, which sports his preferred shade of blue.

He answered that he wanted to go with a site themed in dark mode, which came which at no surprise to me. The accent color for his website was to be a particular shade of cyan, one that he took preference to several years ago when Montreal's public transit got a rebranding, sporting that same color.

Our discussion led to us deciding on three final colors for the website. The website is to be themed with a dark shade of grey, with light textâ€”though not completely white. And the site would only have one accent color, which is the signature shade of blue mentioned earlier. I did consider adding a second accent color of a red-orange variant, which was to be sparsely used throughout the site in order to add a bit of contrast, but we ultimately decided against it.
