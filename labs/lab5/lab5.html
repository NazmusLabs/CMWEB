My Story of Experiencing Halo for the First Time

I was always a gamer at heart, as far as I can remember. But one genre of games for which I never saw the appeal of was the First-Person Shooter (FPS) genre. This was not because I had any moral stance against them; I just simply didn’t enjoy it. But that all changed when I decided to give Halo a try, seventeen years after it was released.
